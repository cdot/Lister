Lists

Items

Items are checked by touching the check box (or optionally by a touch anywhere on the row). The check box can be placed on either side of the display.

A long touch on an item lets you rename it or delete it.

Unless alphabetic sorting is forced, a handle on each item lets you move the item to another position in the list. Touch and hold to drag the item.

Action Bar
